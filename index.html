Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?

We accept most major commercial plans, Medicare, and many state plans. Bring your insurance card to your first visit, and call the front desk if you'd like us to verify coverage in advance.

Are walk-ins available?

Yes, walk-ins are accepted for acute concerns (sudden illness, minor injuries) when an opening is available. For physicals, chronic-care follow-up, and lab work, please schedule ahead.

What should I bring to my first visit?

A photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications and dosages, and any recent lab work or specialist notes you have copies of.

How do I get a prescription refill?

Call the front desk, message your provider through the patient portal, or have your pharmacy send a refill request. Most non-controlled refills are handled within one business day.

How do I get my records released?

Fill out a release-of-information form at the front desk or download it from the patient portal. We can send records electronically or by secure fax.

Do you see children?

Yes. We provide well-child visits, immunizations, sports physicals, and same-day sick visits for children of all ages.
